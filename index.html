About

Nelson is a course instructor at G4H Consult, where he imparts knowledge and guides beginners into programming and information technology. He also founded Wayne Tech and Co, which provides home and workplace services for ICT equipment and gadgets throughout Nigeria.

After many trials in life, as well as experiences from mentors and friends, he feels that truly succeeding in our own route of life cannot be tough unless we are blindfolded folded by ourselves.

One of the ways he has stayed on track is by keeping his eyes on the broad picture while keeping one foot on the gas pedal. Although greater things can only be accomplished by faith, we must tread carefully so that we do not lead ourselves to defeat.
